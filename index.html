About Rohit Sharma

Our legend. Our Hitman. Our record-breaker.One of Aamchi Mumbai’s favourite sons, Rohit Sharma sprung into focus during the inaugural World T20 in 2007, playing a massive role in India winning the trophy.
* Four T20I centuries – the most by a batter in the format
* Three ODI double-centuries - the only batter to hold this record
* Five centuries in a single World Cup edition (2019) - the only batter to hold this record
* Six IPL trophies (Five with MI, one with Deccan Chargers) - the most by any player.
